CHOOSING THE PATH OF WEB

June 8 , 2023

oh god!My first simple website is almost done.

Before three weeks, at the beginning of my vacation, I explored many online events and internships. I was going through social media when a message popped up on my phone. It is an online team learning event from UPSKILL 2.0, presented by TinkerHub , LBSITW. I decided to register for the event "Web Development" with my friends. We form a team called "miracle engineers," with four members, including me. Later, I got a response from my mentor. She added me to a WhatsApp group and conducted a Google Meet, which helped us get a basic idea about the event. It is an event for self-learning about web development.

They prefer a YouTube channel for web development. Our team started to learn the basics of web development by ourselves. Then we slowly move on to the HTML code and CSS. I glad to say that i learn a new language. After one week, they give us a project to make a website. We successfully completed and submitted our simple website. It was not a perfect professional website; it was like a beginner-level website. Then they gave us work to do to make a blog. so we are all busy preparing the blog. Finally, we are preparing our blog, which will be our first. We all hope this will be the best start of our careers.

Of course, we had an amazing experience through the event, and we are glad to have joined this event. Really, UPSKILL was a good learning platform. This vacation is our new start. we are glad to be a part of UPSKILL. Now we are trying to make our first website more beautiful. Ohh!!!, we completed our first blog. Okay it's time to host this blog on GitHub.
